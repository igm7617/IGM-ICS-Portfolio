About me

My name is Iván Gómez, I was born in the city of Racine, Wisconsin to a Mexican mother from the city of León, Guanajuato, and a Chicano (Mexican slang for "Mexican-American") father. I have an older brother and two younger sisters, who are more like nieces to me in this point in life, due to the age gap of 8 and 15 years. When it was just my brother and I, our family quickly moved down to Kenosha and constantly kept moving throughout my younger years. My parent's complicated relationship on top of being "unique" in every enviornment I was in played a toll on my social interaction, mental health, and self-esteem. Regardless, I had my own ways to get through these times, good and bad, but the most important and healthy thing that got me through it were my best friends and my deep love for my (mainly nerdy) passions, which most are still are a part of my life to this day.

I met my best friends, Casey and Cody Cox *uhuhuhuhuhuh* back in 2009 when we were only 7, and we've been through everything since. They ended up moving in 2018 to Surprise, Arizona, a suburb of Phoenix, but we still keep in contact almost everyday. We're simliar and different
